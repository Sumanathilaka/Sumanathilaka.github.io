NLPAICS by UCREL Lancaster University

July 27, 2024

Excited to have presented my paper on "Can LLMs Assist with Ambiguity?" at NLPAICS 2024 in Lancaster! It was a great opportunity to explore how large language models can tackle the challenges of ambiguity in language. Looking forward to further discussions and collaborations in this evolving field!. A big Thanks to my supervisors/coauthors of the work Dr Nicholas Micallef and Dr Julian Hough for the input on this work. Thank you Lancaster university for organizing this amazing program.

ICIIT Conclave on LLM and Generative AI

It was a great pleasure to present my ongoing research on Word Sense Disambiguation using a Large Language Model-based approach at the ICIIT Conclave 2024. This work is co-authored by Dr. Nicholas Micallef and Dr. Julian Hough. I would like to extend my gratitude to the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT) for their innovative idea of conducting the conference on LLMs.
